    Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ's)

    What is the Vendor Panel?

    The Vendor Panel is where external vendors can manage their contracts, performance ratings, and compliance notifications.

    What are Purchase Orders?

    Purchase Orders are formal requests for the purchase of goods or services, which are tracked and managed by procurement teams.

    How do I track vendor performance?

    Vendors are evaluated based on their performance, including timely deliveries, contract adherence, and feedback from the procurement team.

    What is compliance notification?

    Compliance notifications alert vendors and teams about important certification renewals and other regulatory requirements.

    How do I renew a contract?

    Contracts can be renewed by the contract manager based on automated reminders for upcoming expiration dates.

    What is the Procurement Manager Panel?

    The Procurement Manager Panel allows users to manage purchase orders, vendor relationships, and evaluate vendor performance.

    What is the role of a Department Head?

    The Department Head is responsible for initiating purchase orders, tracking departmental budgets, and assigning tasks to team members.

    How do I manage the Budget?

    The Budget Manager tracks the overall budget allocation, monitors expenses, and ensures that funds are distributed properly across departments.

    What are contract renewal reminders?

    Contract renewal reminders are automated alerts that notify managers and vendors when contracts are nearing expiration, ensuring timely renewals.

    How does the system track compliance?

    Compliance is tracked through audit logs, vendor certification checks, and notifications for necessary compliance updates or actions.

    What is the role of a Compliance Officer?

    The Compliance Officer ensures that vendors and contracts meet legal and regulatory standards by conducting audits and managing compliance issues.

    How are Purchase Orders approved?

    After being created by the Department Head, purchase orders are routed to the Procurement Manager for approval or rejection based on business needs.

    What is the Budget Allocation process?

    Budget Allocation involves distributing funds to different departments, with final approval managed by the Budget Manager to ensure financial control.

    How do I view contract history?

    Contract history can be accessed via the Contract Manager's dashboard, which includes a list of all previous contracts and compliance audit logs.

    How do I manage performance evaluations?

    Performance evaluations are based on feedback from various departments and are used to assess vendor quality, delivery, and contract fulfillment.

    How do I create a contract?

    Contracts can be created using predefined templates, with terms and conditions customized to suit the requirements of both parties.

    How do I monitor vendor compliance?

    Vendor compliance is monitored through periodic audits, certification verifications, and checking adherence to contractual terms and regulatory standards.

    What is the Budget Monitoring process?

    Budget monitoring is done through regular expense tracking, analyzing variances, and adjusting budget allocations to prevent overspending.

    How can I receive contract renewal notifications?

    Contract renewal notifications are automatically sent to the Contract Manager when a contract is approaching its expiration date, ensuring timely action.

    How do I assign tasks to team members?

    Tasks can be assigned to team members through the Department Head panel, where tasks are tracked and prioritized based on project requirements.
